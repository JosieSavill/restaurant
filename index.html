The dining area have been conceived in an unusual way by surrounding the
central open dining area facing the river view by raised platforms where
we created a series of semi private dining rooms, a solution that
maintain the privacy of the guests but maintaining the possibility to
appreciate the outstanding view on the bund.
There is something romantic about a formal dining room. Perhaps it is
the thought of having a room completely devoted to eating and
entertaining, or the idea of enjoying a sit-down dinner with family
and friends. Whatever the reason, here are some ideas for bringing
dramatic style to the space.
